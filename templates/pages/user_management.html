Role Assignment Guide
Admin

Manages all users, agencies, system config, audit logs, and platform health.

Incident Commander

Leads incident response, assigns tasks to agencies, monitors progress, and makes strategic decisions.

Agency Coordinator

Receives assignments, coordinates personnel, and reports status for agencies (e.g., Fire Dept, Police, MDRRMO).

EOC Staff

Monitors all incidents, analyses incoming data, and produces reports across the entire command dashboard.

Field Responder

Executes tasks, submits field reports, and uploads media (Rescue personnel, firefighters, medical teams).

Citizen

Reports incidents, receives alerts, tracks incident status, and uses the SOS button.
